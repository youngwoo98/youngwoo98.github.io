About the military duty in Republic of Korea

Conscription in South Korea

The military conscription in South Korea existed since 1957 and requires male citizens between 18 to 28 to join and serve their duty.

The length varies depending on military branch. It's from 18 months to 22 months.

After their duty, they get placed on the reserve roster and are obligated to attend annual training for 5 years.

  • Navy
  • Air Force
